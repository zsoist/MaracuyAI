How to use

Local demo steps

  1. Allow microphone access in your browser.
  2. Put your phone near the Mac microphone.
  3. Press record and play the bird sample from your phone.
  4. Stop recording and wait for the result board.

Useful details

  • Guest mode is created automatically in this browser.
  • A default local bird profile named Maracuya is created automatically.
  • The dashboard runs entirely against your local backend at localhost:8000.
