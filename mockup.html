Budgerigar Care Guide

Everything you need to keep your budgie happy, healthy, and thriving.

🦜

Know Your Budgie

▼

Intelligent social parrots with complex vocal communication. Lifespan 7-15 years.

🏠

Ideal Habitat Setup

▼

Cage environment directly impacts health, mood, and vocalization patterns.

🥦

Nutrition Essentials

▼

Seed-only diets cause malnutrition. Pellets 60-70% + fresh veggies daily.

🧠

Behavior Decoder

▼

Understanding body language helps catch problems early.

Emergency: See a Vet Immediately

  • Sitting on cage floor, fluffed up, eyes closed
  • Open-mouth breathing or persistent tail bobbing
  • Seizures or inability to perch
  • Bleeding that does not stop within 5 minutes
Keep bird warm (85F/29C), quiet dark space, transport to avian vet ASAP.
